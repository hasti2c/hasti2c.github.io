I have a HBSc from the University of Toronto (U of T), with a Computer Science Specialist, Mathematics Major, and Linguistics Minor. Since September 2024, I've been working as a Software Engineer (fancy job title: Forward Deployed AI Engineer) at PolyAI.

Since my last year at U of T, I've been working with Professor Ningning Xie on a type theory research project. Our paper has been accepted at ESOP 2026. Earlier, I worked on two NLP research projects with Professor Annie En-Shiun Lee, published in NAACL 2024 Student Research Workshop and EACL Findings 2024.

My research interests are in theoretical computer science and programming languages theory. More specifically, I'm interested in complexity theory, randomized algorithms, and type theory, but there are many (perhaps too many) other topics I would like to learn more about.
